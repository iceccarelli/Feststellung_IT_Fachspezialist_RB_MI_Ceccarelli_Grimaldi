This repository serves as a verifiable, production-ready package to document and certify the role and qualifications of **Vincenzo Ceccarelli Grimaldi** as an IT Fachspezialist:in within the Regionalbereich Mitte (RB MI) of DB InfraGO AG, specifically for Stations and Services (I.IP-MI-IW 1).

**Mission:** To provide a transparent, cloneable, and verifiable proof of certification, detailing the professional background, responsibilities, and developmental measures, linking this expertise to broader initiatives such as the renewables migration.

## My Role: Electrical Engineer at DB InfraGO AG Stations & Services

As an electrical engineer (Wirtschaftselektrotechniker, RWTH Aachen 2022), my role at DB InfraGO AG Stations & Services (I.IP-MI-IW 1) is centered on the critical IT systems that underpin the safe and efficient operation of passenger stations. This involves a deep understanding and application of **Ril guidelines (813, 513, 861, 114)**, meticulous planning across all phases, and stringent quality control to ensure the integrity and safety of rail infrastructure IT.

My responsibilities include:
*   **IT Safety:** Ensuring that all IT systems and operations comply with the highest safety standards, preventing any potential hazards to personnel, passengers, or the environment.
*   **Planning:** Providing expert technical consultation during all planning phases of IT projects, from conceptualization to implementation, adhering to process modules like MP02-01, LP04, LP05, UP05, and PHB Bau.
*   **Quality Control:** Conducting rigorous quality checks throughout all planning phases and prior to the commissioning of IT systems, including managing VOB/VOL, Nachtragsforderungen, and Mängelverfolgung.
*   **Operational Organization:** Contributing to the efficient organization of IT operations within the regional area, ensuring seamless integration and functionality.
